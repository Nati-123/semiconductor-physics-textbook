# Physics and Math Foundations

## Summary

This chapter reviews the electrostatics, thermal physics, and photon concepts that the rest of the course builds on. Students revisit Coulomb's law, electric field and potential, thermal equilibrium, and the concept of photon energy. These ideas are the physical vocabulary used throughout the course to describe forces, energy, and thermal behavior inside a semiconductor crystal.

## Concepts Covered

This chapter covers the following 8 concepts from the learning graph:

1. Coulomb's Law
2. Electric Field
3. Electric Potential
4. Electrostatic Potential Energy
5. Kinetic Theory of Gases
6. Boltzmann Constant
7. Thermal Equilibrium
8. Photon Energy
