# Diffusion and Advanced Transport Phenomena

## Summary

This chapter builds out the transport picture with the Hall effect (and its coefficient and voltage), the diffusion coefficient, and the Einstein relation that connects diffusion to mobility. Students learn Fick's law and concentration gradients, combine drift and diffusion into a total current density expression, and see how velocity saturation and temperature affect carrier mobility at high fields and elevated temperatures.
