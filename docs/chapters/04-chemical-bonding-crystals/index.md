# Chemical Bonding in Semiconductor Crystals

## Summary

This chapter explains how atoms bond together to form crystalline solids, covering covalent, ionic, and metallic bonding. Students see how silicon's tetrahedral covalent bonding, built from its valence electrons, gives rise to the diamond lattice structure introduced in the previous chapter, and briefly considers amorphous, polycrystalline, and defective crystal forms.
