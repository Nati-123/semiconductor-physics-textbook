# Chapters

This textbook is organized into 18 chapters covering 200 concepts.

## Chapter Overview

1. [Physics and Math Foundations](01-physics-math-foundations/index.md) - Reviews electrostatics, thermal physics, and photon concepts needed for later chapters.
2. [Quantum Mechanics Foundations](02-quantum-mechanics-foundations/index.md) - Introduces wave-particle duality, the Schrödinger equation, and simple quantum systems.
3. [Crystal Lattices and Structures](03-crystal-lattices-structures/index.md) - Covers crystal lattices, unit cells, lattice types, and Miller indices.
4. [Chemical Bonding in Semiconductor Crystals](04-chemical-bonding-crystals/index.md) - Covers covalent, ionic, and metallic bonding and tetrahedral bonding in silicon.
5. [Quantum Mechanics of Periodic Crystals](05-quantum-mechanics-periodic-crystals/index.md) - Derives how a periodic crystal potential produces energy bands.
6. [Band Structure and the Fermi Level](06-band-structure-fermi-level/index.md) - Covers direct/indirect band gaps, effective mass, density of states, and the Fermi level.
7. [Intrinsic and Extrinsic Semiconductors](07-intrinsic-extrinsic-semiconductors/index.md) - Introduces intrinsic vs. extrinsic material and donor/acceptor doping.
8. [Doping, Ionization, and Temperature Regimes](08-doping-ionization-temperature/index.md) - Covers n-type/p-type doping, dopant ionization, and temperature regions.
9. [Carrier Concentration Statistics](09-carrier-concentration-statistics/index.md) - Introduces free electrons, holes, and the Fermi-Dirac distribution.
10. [Fermi Level Position and Carrier Equations](10-fermi-level-carrier-equations/index.md) - Derives electron/hole concentration equations and locates the Fermi level.
11. [Drift Current and Carrier Mobility](11-drift-current-mobility/index.md) - Covers drift and diffusion current, mobility, scattering, and conductivity.
12. [Diffusion and Advanced Transport Phenomena](12-diffusion-transport-phenomena/index.md) - Covers the Hall effect, the Einstein relation, and advanced transport behavior.
13. [Non-Equilibrium Carriers and Recombination](13-non-equilibrium-carriers-recombination/index.md) - Introduces excess carrier generation/recombination and the continuity equation.
14. [The P-N Junction at Equilibrium](14-pn-junction-equilibrium/index.md) - Builds the equilibrium p-n junction: depletion region, built-in potential, and capacitance.
15. [The P-N Junction Under Bias](15-pn-junction-under-bias/index.md) - Covers forward/reverse bias, the ideal diode equation, and breakdown.
16. [Metal-Semiconductor and MOS Junctions](16-metal-semiconductor-mos-junctions/index.md) - Covers Schottky barriers, ohmic contacts, and the MOS capacitor.
17. [Optical and Thermal Properties of Semiconductors](17-optical-thermal-properties/index.md) - Covers light absorption and optoelectronic devices.
18. [Semiconductor Devices and Applications](18-semiconductor-devices-applications/index.md) - Synthesizes applied devices and the capstone device project.

## How to Use This Textbook

Chapters are ordered so that every concept appears after all of its prerequisites — work through them in numerical order on a first pass. Each chapter's "Prerequisites" section lists the specific earlier chapters it builds on, so if you already have a strong background in a topic (e.g., quantum mechanics or crystal structure), you can use those links to check what you can skim.

**Note:** Each chapter includes a list of concepts covered. Make sure to complete prerequisites before moving to advanced chapters.
