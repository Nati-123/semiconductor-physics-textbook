# Semiconductor Devices and Applications

## Summary

This capstone chapter connects the course's physics to applied devices: power diodes, rectifier circuits, and varactor diodes, along with an introduction to bipolar transistor and MOSFET basics. Students consider semiconductor device modeling and simulation at a conceptual level, practice constructing band diagrams, and weigh device design trade-offs before completing a capstone project that models the physics of a real semiconductor device.

## Concepts Covered

This chapter covers the following 10 concepts from the learning graph:

1. Power Diode
2. Rectifier Circuit
3. Varactor Diode
4. Bipolar Transistor Basics
5. MOSFET Basics
6. Semiconductor Device Modeling
7. Device Simulation Concept
8. Band Diagram Construction
9. Device Design Trade-Offs
10. Capstone Device Project
