# Carrier Concentration Statistics

## Summary

This chapter introduces free electrons and holes as the two carrier types in a semiconductor, and applies the Fermi-Dirac distribution and density of states function to describe how carriers populate energy states. Students derive the effective density of states and the intrinsic carrier concentration, and use the mass action law and charge neutrality condition to relate carrier populations to doping.
