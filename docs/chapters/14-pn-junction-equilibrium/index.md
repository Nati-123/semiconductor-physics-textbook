# The P-N Junction at Equilibrium

## Summary

This chapter builds the p-n junction from first principles at thermal equilibrium, starting from the metallurgical junction and Poisson's equation, through the depletion approximation, to the built-in potential and depletion charge density. Students calculate the junction electric field, depletion width, and junction capacitance — the equilibrium quantities needed before introducing bias in the next chapter.
