# The P-N Junction Under Bias

## Summary

This chapter analyzes the p-n junction away from equilibrium, covering forward and reverse bias, minority carrier injection, and the short-base and long-base diode approximations. Students derive the saturation current and the ideal diode equation, then study reverse breakdown, including avalanche and Zener mechanisms, and summarize the complete junction I-V characteristic.
