# Quantum Mechanics Foundations

## Summary

This chapter introduces the quantum-mechanical concepts required to understand solids: wave-particle duality, the Heisenberg uncertainty principle, and the Schrödinger equation. Students work through simple quantum systems — the particle in a box, potential wells, and tunneling — that establish the mathematical tools used later to derive energy bands.
