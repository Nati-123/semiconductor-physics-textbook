# Fermi Level Position and Carrier Equations

## Summary

This chapter uses the Boltzmann approximation to simplify carrier statistics in nondegenerate semiconductors, deriving working equations for electron and hole concentration. Students learn how the Fermi level position shifts with doping and temperature, and locate the intrinsic Fermi level as a reference point, arriving at the general carrier concentration equations used throughout the rest of the course.
