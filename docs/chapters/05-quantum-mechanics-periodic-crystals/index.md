# Quantum Mechanics of Periodic Crystals

## Summary

This chapter applies quantum mechanics to a periodic crystal potential, using Bloch's theorem and the Kronig-Penney model to show how discrete atomic energy levels broaden into continuous energy bands. Students learn the reciprocal lattice and Brillouin zone concepts used to describe these bands, and see how allowed and forbidden energy regions emerge from the crystal's periodicity.
